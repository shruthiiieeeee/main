Brain Tumor

A brain tumor is an abnormal growth of cells in the brain or the central spinal canal. These tumors can be benign (non-cancerous) or malignant (cancerous). While benign tumors do not spread to other parts of the body, they can still cause serious health issues by pressing on surrounding brain tissues. Malignant tumors, on the other hand, can invade nearby tissues and spread to other parts of the body through the bloodstream and lymphatic system.
